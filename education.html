Patient Health Tips

Diabetes Management

  • Check blood sugar levels daily as instructed by your doctor.
  • Maintain a balanced diet with low sugar and complex carbs.
  • Exercise for at least 30 minutes every day.
  • Take prescribed medications on time.

Heart Health

  • Monitor blood pressure regularly.
  • Avoid excessive salt and fatty foods.
  • Engage in mild to moderate physical activity.
  • Stay hydrated and manage stress levels.

Respiratory Health

  • Avoid exposure to smoke or pollutants.
  • Practice breathing exercises daily.
  • Follow doctor’s instructions for inhalers or medications.
  • Keep indoor air clean and humidified if necessary.

General Wellness

  • Get 7–8 hours of sleep every night.
  • Maintain hydration by drinking sufficient water.
  • Eat a balanced diet with fruits, vegetables, and proteins.
  • Practice stress-reduction techniques like meditation or yoga.
